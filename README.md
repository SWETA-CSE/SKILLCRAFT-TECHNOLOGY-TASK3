Task 3 Completed | Business Card Design

Sharing my third task from my Graphic Design Internship at SkillCraft Technology.

For this task, I designed a two-sided business card for Homefolk, a sustainable home décor and handmade-living brand.

I continued the brand’s earthy visual identity by using deep green, soft cream, leaf illustrations, and warm brown accents. The front side introduces the brand and its purpose, while the back side keeps the contact details clear, organised, and easy to read.

My focus was to create a business card that feels professional while still carrying the calm, natural, and handmade personality of Homefolk.

This task helped me practise balancing branding with usability—making sure the design looks attractive but also communicates important information quickly.

Tools used: Canva and Freepik
